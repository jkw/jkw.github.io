Mosquitoes?!

Published: Thu 24 May 2018
Updated: Thu 24 May 2018
By jkw

In Blog.

tags: blog life

Many things can interrupt you during your thoughtfoul hours. But there is one thing that is extra annoying and can interrupt your workflow tremendeously! And that is one thing we have right now in Sweden, mosquitoes!

Sitting in the darkness doing your regular programming, youtubing or whatever and all of the sudden you hear that annoying sound they make. AND BAM! They sting you!

Itching, itching and more itching. It can continue for hours even days.

So my night has not been nice at all. I lost count after killing thirty mosquitoes!
